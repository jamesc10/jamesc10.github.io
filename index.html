My Hometown
Owensboro KY

Power Generation

This interactive map shows the locations, energy by fuel and relative output for United States Power Stations that use two specified types of fuel.

Living Paleo

Meat & vegatables are the heart of the paleo diet. By combining beef cattle production with density of farmers' markets, this interactive map shows good places to live.

Southern Appalachian Trail

This map uses a geo-referenced base map created by Erwin Raisz for a classic cartography look. The trail data was collected from a GPS unit.

Denver Amenities

Quarry Stockpiles

UAV's and photogrammetry software create a 3D model that can be used to calculate the volume of a stockpile. The PDF is part of the customer deliverable package.

About me

I'm James Coil, I'm new to Web Mapping but the New Maps Plus program at UK has filled me with knowledge of Leaflet, JavaScript, CSS and HTML and I'm ready to go. I'm specializing in the use of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) collected mapping data.

If I'm not flying UAV's or making cool maps, I put a lot of effort into staying healthy by eating well and exercising (a hobby of sorts), I also like darts, live music and shaving with a straight razor.
